This is a jQuery Mobile-based website made for fun, where I write down my characters' info and builds. Not all of them are final builds, some are stil under study and may change at any moment.

I believe each build must reflect the player's way of playing, including habits and taste. So, there isn't the best build of all, but the best build for each person. I am rather aggressive in my way of playing, mostly. So my builds reflect that. I encourage everyone to make their own builds ;)
