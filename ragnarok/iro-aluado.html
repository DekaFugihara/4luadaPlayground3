Warp Portal

Open a portal at the selected cell to a memorized map or to your save point. It remains opened for the skill duration or until you enter it, and while it is open, anyone can go into it. You can memorize a warping spot using the 

/memo
command. You can memorize up to 3 spots, the fourth will erase the first one.

  • Target: Ground
  • Cost: 26 SP, 1 Blue Gemstone
  • Area: 1x1 cell

  • Duration: 50s
