Drow Skills

Drow Stategy

Attack fast and hard. Move even faster. Kill, kill, kill. A very offensive build. I didn't even put a BKB there. I am sure you'll remember when (of if) the time comes. Depending on your items, you focus on the first skill or basic attack.

Tango

Essencial to recover some HP during early game.

Close

Ring of Protection

This raises your armor and is necessary to build Basilius (and Aquilla afterwards).

Close

Slippers of Agility

Raise your attack. This is also required to build Aquilla.

Close

Phase Boots

A boot to chase down your enemies and shoot them down!

Close

Ring of Aquila

Keep up your mana to slow down your enemies with the first skill to the death.

Close

Helm of Dominator

This will heal you up, and help you stack! But you must choose between this or Mask of Madness.

Close

Aghanim Scepter

Causes Drow Ranger's attacks to splinter on the target, hitting nearby units with normal attacks that deal reduced (50%) damage. Kill more creeps, get more money, get stronger!

Close

Crystalys

Raise your damage with criticals! Lets kill faster!

Close

Sange and Yasha

Move faster and make you foe slower!

Close

Daedalus

If you have a Crystalys, why not evolve it to Daedalus and raise our damage and critical rate?

Close

Hand of Midas

Great early? Get a Hand of Midas and prepare to have a great Mid and Late too (possibly)!

Close

Mask of Madness

If you miss the attack speed and prefer farming heroes instead of creeps, maybe you should get Madness instead of Dominator. Just maybe. Test it out!

Close

Dragon Lance

Kill your enemies even when they outrun you... IF they outrun you.

Close

Monkey King Bar

Raise your damage and attack speed, and with this, you have a chance to mini-stun. But you can't use your first skill if you want to bash. This combos with Dragon Lance great.

Close

Desolator

This is a great option to Satanic/Dominator, specially if you got Madress instead. But two of this in the same team is a waste.

Close

Silver Edge

Not my style to get an item to turn invisible. But this may be useful since it may slows down foes. And this item may help you scape death.

Close

Manta Style

Provides mobilitym stats and a chance to scape certain abilities thrown at you. Besides, the illusions benefit from Drow's ult. What more to like?

Close

Diffusal Blade

Drain you enemie's mana while giving more damage and slowing him/her down.

Close

Butterfly

Damage and attack speed, evasion or move speed... What more do you want?

Close

Travel Boots

Get to the fight (almost) instantly, or push fast and effectively.

Close

Moon Shard

Attack speed, attack speed, attack speed...

Close

Viscous Nasal Goo

Reduce armor and movement speed. Spam a lot while chasing someone down.

Close

Quill Spray

Use this to kill creep waves, and in all fights. Its deals bonus damage each time a unit is hit by it in the last 14 seconds.

Close

Bristleback

Passive. Bristleback takes less damage from the sides and the back. Each 230 damage from the rear, Bristleback automatically uses Quill Spray of the current level.

Close

Warpath

Passive. Every time Bristleback casts a spell, his damage and movement speed are improved for some time, and it stacks!

Close
