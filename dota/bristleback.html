Bristleback Skills

Bristleback Stategy

This hero is a tanker. The strategy is simple: survive and hit. Or survive and run away. Don't forget to change your Treads as needed.

Tango

Essencial to recover some HP during early game, while your first skill won't heal much. And you'd rather use your mana for offense.

Close

Healing Salve

Use it when HP is too low, when the lane is too aggressive.

Close

Stout Shield

This shield will help you tank on your lane.

Close

Power Treads

Make the best of this changing its stat. INT (blue) will improve mana recovery. STR (red) increases damage, max HP and HP recovery. AGI (green) improves your attack speed.

Close

Vanguard

Evolve stout shield to this. It increases max HP and HP regeneration. It also blocks enemies's attacks. Very helpful for a tanker like Bristleback.

Close

Blade Mail

Increase defense, damage and reflect damage all in one item? This is a must!

Close

Hood of Defiance

You already have physical resistance, and now magic resistance!

Close

Skull Basher

You are a tanker, but you can hit! So hit, and stun if you can!

Close

Assault Cuirass

Yes, you can hit, and it's better if you hit faster! Assault also improves you defense and your allies's attack speed.

Close

Heart of Tarrasque

Life is low most of the time? Get a Tarrasque. It improves your Max HP and HP recovery. You can leave it for later if you're OK.

Close

Abyssal Blade

You don't have a disabling skill. Until now! Use this item to stun from distance.

Close

Shiva's Guard

Slow down your enemies around you while giving them some damage! Run after them and bash them to death! Mwahaha!

Close

Pipe of Insight

Prepare your team for a fight, raising everyone's magical resistance. Only make this item if there's enough magical heroes as enemies.

Close

Crimson Guard

Protect yourself and your friends from physical attacks. Only make this item if there's enough melee heroes as your enemies.

Close

Viscous Nasal Goo

Reduce armor and movement speed. Spam a lot while chasing someone down.

Close

Quill Spray

Use this to kill creep waves, and in all fights. Its deals bonus damage each time a unit is hit by it in the last 14 seconds.

Close

Bristleback

Passive. Bristleback takes less damage from the sides and the back. Each 230 damage from the rear, Bristleback automatically uses Quill Spray of the current level.

Close

Warpath

Passive. Every time Bristleback casts a spell, his damage and movement speed are improved for some time, and it stacks!
