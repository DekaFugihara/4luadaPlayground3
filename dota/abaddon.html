Abaddon Stategy

Carry Abaddon

Abadon has a high survivability, besides he slows down the enemies he strikes, which makes him a great chaser. That means Sange and Yasha and Phase Boots to run faster, Skadi and/or Shivas ti slow down enemies even more, and Assault Cuirass to attack faster.

Support Abaddon

As support, he will need a lot of mana, so Url will be a lot of help, and so will Soul Ring - specially having Abaddon's first skill healing him back. Soul RIng would be enough for Abaddon, but Urn is great if no one else on the team is making it. Mekansm ir great to help him survive a little longer as well as his allies.
